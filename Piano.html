Piano

I play piano. Semi competetently.

I've been learning classical piano since I was 6 years old. As I grew older, and started watching TV, Movies, and Anime, I started experimenting with playing the soundtracks of my favorite media on my piano. I've participated in numerous competitions and played in venues such as the Westminster Conservatory of Music, Kosciuszko Hall, and Carnegie Hall. I made a few piano covers and uploaded them to my YouTube channel.
